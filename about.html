About Our Company And What We Offer

Paryatak guide is a leading sales training institute and trip provider in India, founded by Divesh Dubey in 2024. Initially, it offered small and medium tour packages through its web and app platforms. The company covers a wide range of travel services, including accommodations like hotels and B&Bs, attractions such as theme parks and museums, and various transportation options like car rentals and cruises.

At Paryatak guide, we pride ourselves on an expanding network of leisure resorts, clubs, hotels, and wellness centers across top holiday destinations in India and abroad. We are a passionate team of creative travel enthusiasts who design tailor-made holidays and experiences for travelers around the globe.

With an inventory of over 2000 rooms spread across 45+ partner resorts, and access to 4500 exchange resorts in 100+ countries, we offer unmatched variety and flexibility.

Our travel categories include:

  • India Holidays
  • International Holidays
  • Honeymoon Packages (Domestic & International)
  • Africa, Asia, Australia, Europe, New Zealand, and USA Tour Packages
  • Special Offers on International and Domestic Tours
